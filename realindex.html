I've been a personal trainer for the past 5 years, following 6 years as a CrossFit coach. During this time, I've had the privilege of working with seniors and school-aged children. My dedication to health and fitness is unwavering; I believe it's unique to each person. You don't have to be the strongest in the room, just stronger than yesterday. My journey began 12 years ago, focused on both physical and mental health. Starting in a CrossFit gym, I competed and grew as an athlete. Later, I immersed myself in Olympic weightlifting, achieving a state championship medal and competing nationally. After welcoming another baby, I've returned with a renewed perspective. Today, I view health and fitness as a lifelong commitment. Hydration, nutrition, daily movement, and resistance training are non-negotiables. As a mother of three, I understand the challenges of balancing these priorities. My extensive experience and personal journey have taught me how to integrate fitness into everyday life seamlessly. Working with me means focusing on becoming the best version of yourself.
